Project information

  • We propose a deep learning-based method for the lung and its nodule segmentation from computed tomography (CT) scan images. For a clinical diagnosis, lung nodule segmentation and detection are crucial. Lung Cancer is an infectious lung tumor caused by uncontrollable tissue growth in the lungs. This paper presents a deep learning-based approach for lung segmentation from CT scan images. we cover lung CT segmentation using the fully convolutional network method, and after that segmented the nodules part from lung CT.
  • The accuracy for segmenting the lungs from CT scan images is 96.16%. These results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method in accurately segmenting lung nodules from CT scan images.
  • After segmenting the lung part we segment the nodules present in the lungs by using U-Net deep learning model, dice coefficient comes in training at 0.87, and for testing, it is 0.68.
